## Practical Tips for Your Eclectic Outdoor Wedding

1. **Choose durable florals:** Real flowers can droop in heat or get crushed in transit. Wholesale silk flowers are your best friend for outdoor venues.
2. **Lighting matters:** String lights, lanterns, and candles create a magical evening atmosphere. Place them near your flower wall to highlight the textures.
3. **Embrace personal style:** This couple wore sneakers and a blue suit. Don't be afraid to mix formal with casual—your wedding should feel like you.
4. **Plan for weather:** Have a backup plan for rain, but silk flowers won't wilt if they get wet. They're a practical choice for unpredictable climates.
