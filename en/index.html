Our Production Process

From raw materials to finished products — every step is quality-controlled in our factory.

🎯
Step 1

Design & Molding

Professional designers create petal molds based on real flower species. Precision molding ensures lifelike shape and texture.

🎨
Step 2

Material Selection & Dyeing

High-grade silk, polyester, and eco-friendly materials selected. Each batch is hand-dyed to achieve natural, fade-resistant colors.

🤲
Step 3

Hand Assembly

Skilled craftsmen hand-assemble each bloom — layering petals, adding stamens, and shaping every flower for a realistic finish.

✅
Step 4

Quality Check & Packing

Strict quality control on shape, color, and durability. Products are carefully packed for safe international shipping.
