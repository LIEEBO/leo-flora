## Crafting Your Own Purse Bouquet with Artificial Flowers

One of the best things about the purse bouquet is how easy it is to DIY, especially when you use **bridal bouquet artificial flowers**. Real flowers can wilt, stain, or shed petals, but high-quality silk or faux blooms stay perfect all day. Here’s how to make your own:

- **Choose your base**: Start with a small clutch or a purse-shaped frame. You can find these at craft stores or thrift shops for a few dollars.
- **Select your flowers**: Opt for a mix of small blooms like roses, baby’s breath, or lavender. Stick to a color palette that matches your wedding theme.
- **Attach with glue**: Use a hot glue gun to secure the stems to the purse. Layer them for a full, lush look. Add greenery like eucalyptus or ferns for texture.
- **Add personal touches**: Tuck in a small charm, a lace ribbon, or even a photo for a sentimental touch.

This project is perfect for budget-conscious brides seeking **affordable wedding flowers** that don’t compromise on style. A single purse bouquet can cost under $30 to make, compared to $100+ for a custom floral bouquet.
