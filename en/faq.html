Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of artificial flowers do you offer?

We provide a wide range of artificial flowers including silk flowers, wedding flowers, home decoration flowers, and commercial display arrangements. Our products are designed to look realistic and maintain long-lasting beauty.

2. Can I place wholesale orders?

Yes, we accept bulk orders for international clients. We offer competitive wholesale pricing and support OEM/ODM customization based on your requirements.

3. Do you provide customization services?

Absolutely. We can customize flower designs, colors, packaging, and labels according to your brand or project needs. Please contact us with your specific requirements.

4. What materials are your artificial flowers made of?

Our flowers are made from high-quality silk and eco-friendly materials. We ensure realistic texture, colors, and durability in all products.

5. How long does it take to produce an order?

Production time depends on order quantity and customization. For standard wholesale orders, typical production time is 10-20 days. Customized orders may require more time; we will confirm the exact schedule upon order.

6. How do you ship internationally?

We ship worldwide via reliable carriers. Shipping methods include air freight, express courier, and sea freight for bulk orders. Delivery time varies depending on destination and chosen method.

7. How do I get your product catalog?

You can contact us via our contact page to receive the latest catalog and price list.

8. Are your flowers reusable?

Yes, our artificial flowers are designed for long-term use. They can be reused for multiple events or decorative purposes without losing quality.

9. Can you provide samples?

Yes, we can provide samples for evaluation. Sample fees and shipping costs apply. Please contact us with your sample request details.

10. How do I place an order?

Orders can be placed by contacting us directly through our contact page. We will provide a quotation, confirm details, and guide you through the payment and delivery process.
